Company Basics

What does the company do, or offer?

This company (name pending, i'm bad at names D:) is a blacksmithing company aimed towards pracitioners of HEMA and Kendo. Offering various types of blades or weapons that are dull or sharp, as well as offering services for creating custom weapons.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

To potential customers, the company should come across as a reliable blacksmith, capable of delivering quality products for the right price. As well as someone who has a deep understanding of at least HEMA & Kendo, with knowledge of other weapon-based martial arts.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

By being a company catered to specifically HEMA & Japanese swordsmanship, potnetial customers will assume that the company is aware of the standards expected of what they offer. Such as proper weight balancing, quality matching the price. Offering custom work will also build up expectations by expecting high-quality personalized weapons, whether it is a standard blade, or one drawing its origins from fantasy.
