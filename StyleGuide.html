Branding Section

Dungeoner's

What Does Dungeoner's Offer?

Dungeoners is a (modern) fantasy supply shop where adventurers can find all they might need for a quest. It supplies everything from potions and swords to talismans and ancient tombs.

Who is Dungeoner's to Potential Customers? (As a Person)

The company is perceived as friendly, welcoming, and local but there should be a somewhat comedic/mysterious element with how it is able to acquire some of its more dangerous and valuable items. I want the shop to feel very local/small town too. Customer wise, I think Dungeoner's should cater primarily to its local base as well. Maybe there is a dungeon or two just outside of the town or maybe the town is built around one. Either way, customers who shop at Dungeoner's tend to stay customers and are likely trying to get their goods quickly and reliably so they can continue exploring.

Why Dungeoner's?

Dungeoner's is focused on selling products for every price range (with quality increasing with price). That being said, the products at low levels should still be considered good quality which makes it a good store for adventures of all skill levels and coin purse sizes. Maybe they also have interesting weekly specials or limited time only items
